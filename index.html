Presently, Bing Wang is pursuing his Ph.D. degree at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Jilin University (JLU), under the supervision of Ximing Li. Research Interests

My research interests basically lie in SafeTy And Trustworthiness of Internet Content (STATIC).
